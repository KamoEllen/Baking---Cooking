Homemade Bread

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Directions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, and yeast. Stir in warm water and oil until dough comes together.
  2. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until smooth and elastic.
  3. Place dough in a greased bowl and cover with a damp cloth. Set aside in a warm place to rise for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  4. Punch down dough and turn out onto a lightly floured surface. Shape into a loaf and place in a greased 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  5. Cover with a damp cloth and set aside to rise for about 30 minutes, or until dough has risen to the top of the pan.
