Blueberry Muffins

These delicious blueberry muffins are the perfect breakfast or snack. They're bursting with juicy blueberries and have a tender, crumbly texture.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a muffin tin or line with muffin cups.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder. Make a well in the center.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the oil, egg, and milk. Pour into the well in the flour mixture and stir until just combined.
  4. Fold in the blueberries. Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups.
  5. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean. Allow to cool slightly before serving. Enjoy!
