Grilled Cheese Sandwich

A grilled cheese sandwich is the ultimate comfort food. It's simple to make and always hits the spot, whether you're enjoying it for lunch, dinner, or a midnight snack. All you need is some bread, cheese, and a little bit of butter.

To make the perfect grilled cheese sandwich, start by heating a skillet over medium heat. Butter one side of each slice of bread, and place one slice, butter side down, in the skillet. Add a few slices of your favorite cheese, such as cheddar, Swiss, or mozzarella, on top of the bread. Top with the other slice of bread, butter side up.

Cook the sandwich for a few minutes on each side, or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted. Serve the sandwich hot, with a side of soup or a green salad for a complete meal.
