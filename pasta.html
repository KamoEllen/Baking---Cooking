Spaghetti and Meatballs

There's nothing quite like a big bowl of steaming hot spaghetti and meatballs. This classic Italian dish has been a favorite of families and food lovers alike for generations.

To make the perfect spaghetti and meatballs, you'll need some ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, an egg, garlic, salt, and black pepper. Mix these ingredients together in a large bowl until well combined, then shape the mixture into small meatballs. In a separate pot, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions.

Meanwhile, heat some olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the meatballs to the skillet and cook until they are browned on all sides. Reduce the heat to low and add a jar of your favorite marinara sauce to the skillet. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the meatballs are cooked through and the sauce is thick and flavorful.

To serve, spoon the spaghetti onto a plate and top with a few meatballs and a generous helping of the marinara sauce. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and parsley, and enjoy!
