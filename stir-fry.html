Vegetable Stir Fry

A stir fry is a quick and easy meal that's perfect for busy weeknights. This vegetable stir fry is packed with nutrients and can be customized with your favorite veggies. All you need is a wok or large frying pan and a few simple ingredients.

To make a vegetable stir fry, start by heating a tablespoon of oil in a wok or frying pan over high heat. Add your choice of vegetables, such as sliced bell peppers, onions, carrots, and broccoli, and stir fry for a few minutes until they are slightly tender. You can also add in some protein, such as tofu, chicken, or shrimp, if you like.

Once the vegetables are cooked to your liking, make a sauce by mixing together equal parts soy sauce, rice vinegar, and honey. Add the sauce to the wok and toss everything together to coat. Serve the stir fry over a bed of rice or noodles, and enjoy!
